The Sense of Belonging Scale

Chapter 2 The SBS Items: Global-Trait Form

This page contains the 16 items of the SBS in their global form, as well as some descriptive information for them. The SBS-8 is comprised of the first 8 items listed in the Table. Because the scale was validated under a 1-factor model, we recommend administering the scale on a 5 or 7 point Likert-type scale and averaging item responses to derive a total score. In the validation studies, we used this scale for all forms:

  • Strongly disagree
  • Somewhat disagree
  • Neither agree nor disagree
  • Somewhat agree
  • Strongly agree
